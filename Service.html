What is Service page of website

home page is the initial or main page of a website, serving as the starting point for navigation. It provides an overview of the website's content and links to other sections or pages. When you enter a website's URL in a browser, you are typically directed to its home page

  1. It creates the first impression for visitors, influencing their perception of the website.
  2. Navigation: It provides navigation to other parts of the website, often featuring a navigation bar and search box
  3. Content Overview: It offers a summary of the website's content and purpose.
  4. User Engagement: A well-designed home page can attract users and encourage them to explore further, while a poorly designed one can deter visitors
